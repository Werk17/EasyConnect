Inspiration Behind EasyConnect

Welcome to The Family!

 The Inspiration behind EasyConnect is the idea of a message app for businesses. With so much information being exchanged, it is important to have a safe, convenient, and fluid way to communicate within your organization.
We are a team of two students from the University of Montana with a passion for the field of software development. This website is our idealogiy for a message app for businesses.
Each and every person that uses an email address to communicate has recieved phishing emails from hackers. This is a major concern for many businesses. We believe that the best way to protect your business is to protect your employees. The best way to do that is to offer a safe and secure way to communicate.
EasyConnect gives your organization the ability to communicate within itself. It is like a help desk for your employees. Upon sign up and creation, groups are created for your employees. These groups can be accessed by your employees. They are help desk style, and remove the headache of having to search through hundreds of emails to find the right person. Not only that, they also eliminate user error and hackers.
Mission Statement:
  • We at EasyConnect pride our abilities to provide your organization with the communication tools that it needs to succeed. It is our mission and hope that you will use our platform to help your organization be successful in it's endevors.
